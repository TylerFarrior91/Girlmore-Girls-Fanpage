About Gilmore Girls

Gilmore Girls is an American television series created by Amy Sherman-Palladino. The show follows the lives of Lorelai Gilmore and her daughter Rory, as they navigate through the ups and downs of life in the charming town of Stars Hollow.

Main Characters

Some of the main characters in Gilmore Girls include:

  • Lorelai Gilmore
  • Rory Gilmore
  • Luke Danes
  • Emily Gilmore
